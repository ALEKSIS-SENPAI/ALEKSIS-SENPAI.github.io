FAQs for the Lazy

How do I install the mods?

Download the mods, drag it to your mods folder, and voilà! You're done.

Where do I put the models?

Unpack them into C:\Users\yourusername\...\config\yes_steve_model\custom. Easy peasy.

What version of Minecraft is needed?

You’ll need Minecraft 1.20.1 and forge 47.2.0. No exceptions.

Can I get help if needed?

Help? Nah, figure it out yourself.
